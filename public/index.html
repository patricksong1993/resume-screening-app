Frequently Asked Questions

Is it secure?

We use end-to-end encryption while transferring your data.

What file formats do you support?

We only support PDF files for the time being, and will extend to other file types in future.

Can I use this for free?

Yes, this application is still in a demo stage and we will offer a free tier usage once live.

How is my data stored?

We don't sell your data and we don't store your data, as long as a resume is processed, the data is returned to you and we store nothing.

How to screen resumes effectively?

Our AI tool evaluates resumes against job postings, ranking candidates by analyzing their backgrounds and skills alignment.
